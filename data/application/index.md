Prospective students should review the current Institute of Science Tokyo graduate admissions requirements before applying. Admission decisions, deadlines, required documents, and eligibility rules are governed by the university's official information.

## Prospective students

MDL Lab welcomes applicants interested in the theory and methods behind dependable machine learning and artificial intelligence.

- **Master's applicants** — Describe the research questions you want to study and how they relate to the lab's current directions.
- **Doctoral applicants** — Prepare a focused research proposal, your academic background, and relevant publications or research experience.
- **International applicants** — Check the applicable admission route, language requirements, and visa schedule early.

## Before applying

- Review the lab's research directions and recent publications.
- Confirm which graduate program and admission schedule apply to you.
- Prepare a concise explanation of your research interests and relevant technical experience.
- Follow the official university procedure for all formal application documents and deadlines.

## Research fit

Current themes include AI security and privacy, dependable AI, explainability, fairness, robust decision-making, and the security of AI-driven cyber-physical systems.

> Application requirements can change. Always treat the official Institute of Science Tokyo admissions page as the authoritative source.
