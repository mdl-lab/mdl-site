About MDL Lab

Machine learning
we can depend on

MDL Lab is part of the School of Computing's Department of Computer Science / Artificial Intelligence Course at Institute of Science Tokyo. We study dependable machine learning and AI, with particular emphasis on security, privacy, explainability, and fairness.

We also collaborate with external universities on AI-assisted pathological diagnosis of malignant lymphoma and on attacks and security for automated driving systems. The group operates at Institute of Science Tokyo and as the AI Security and Privacy Team at RIKEN AIP.
