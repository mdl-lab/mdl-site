- Obvious spacing and typographical issues from Google Sites were normalized.
- Longer introductory text was paraphrased for the new layout while preserving the original factual scope.
- No custom laboratory photographs were present on the source site; its generic Google Sites header artwork was not copied. The existing original hero artwork remains in use.
